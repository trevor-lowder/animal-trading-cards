Lion

King of the Jungle

A Lion's powerful roar can be heard from up to 5 miles away!

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Size: Males - 10.5ft, Females - 9ft
  • Average Lifespan: Wild - 12yr, Captive - 20yr
  • Diet: Wildebeest, Zebra, Antelope, small game
  • Habitat: Savanna, Grassland, Woodland
  • Locale: Sub-Saharan Africa, Gir Forest of India

Male lions are distinguished by their magnificent manes, which serve as a symbol of dominance and virility. Lions live in social groups called prides, consisting of related females, their offspring, and a small number of adult males.
